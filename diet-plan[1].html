7-Day Weight Loss Diet Plan

weightloss plan

Day 1

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and honey
  • Snack: A small apple
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad
  • Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with steamed broccoli

Day 2

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with sliced banana
  • Snack: A handful of almonds
  • Lunch: Turkey wrap with lettuce and tomato
  • Snack: Celery sticks with peanut butter
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables

Day 3

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, and protein powder
  • Snack: A pear
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with a side salad
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp with quinoa and asparagus

Day 4

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach
  • Snack: A small peach
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with black beans and corn
  • Snack: Sliced bell peppers with guacamole
  • Dinner: Baked chicken breast with sweet potatoes

Day 5

  • Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with almond milk
  • Snack: A small apple or a handful of walnuts
  • Lunch: Spinach and feta stuffed whole-grain pita
  • Snack: Cucumber slices with tzatziki
  • Dinner: Grilled turkey burger (no bun) with mixed vegetables

Day 6

  • Breakfast: Whole-grain toast with avocado and poached egg
  • Snack: A small banana
  • Lunch: Chickpea salad with cherry tomatoes and cucumber
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with a drizzle of honey
  • Dinner: Baked cod with asparagus and brown rice

Day 7

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with mixed berries and granola
  • Snack: A small orange
  • Lunch: Grilled vegetable wrap with hummus
  • Snack: Baby carrots with ranch dip (light)
  • Dinner: Stuffed bell peppers with ground turkey and quinoa

weight gain plan
