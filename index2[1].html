Healthcare Diet Plans

Diet plan

A diet plan is a structured guide that outlines what, when, and how much to eat to achieve specific health, fitness, or weight-related goals. It typically includes:
Food Choices: What types of foods to eat (e.g., lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, vegetables, healthy fats).
Portion Sizes: How much to eat to meet calorie or nutrient needs.
Meal Timing: When to eat (e.g., 3 meals + 2 snacks, intermittent fasting).
Hydration: Guidance on water intake.
Goals: Tailored to weight loss, muscle gain, improved health, or managing medical conditions (e.g., diabetes, high cholesterol).
Types of Diet Plans
Weight Loss: Focuses on calorie deficit (eating fewer calories than burned).
Muscle Gain: High-protein, calorie-surplus plan.
Balanced Diet: General health and maintenance.
Medical Diets: For conditions like diabetes (low-carb), heart health (low-fat), or allergies (gluten-free, dairy-free).
Specialized Diets: Keto, vegan, paleo, Mediterranean, etc.
